About the Firm

Precision is not optional.
It is the standard.

Founded in 2015 and registered with the CAC under RC 2345833, Ben Hirki & Co Chartered Accountants was established with a clear mandate: bring rigorous, internationally aligned financial services to Nigerian businesses that deserve more than mediocre compliance work.

From our head office in Barnawa, Kaduna, and our branch on Ahmadu Bello Way in Yola, Adamawa State, we serve clients across Northern Nigeria and beyond. Every engagement is governed by ICAN standards, a commitment to accuracy, and a genuine interest in client success.
